## 📐 Concept 1: Backwards Design (Output First)
In professional engineering, we don't buy bricks until the blueprint is signed. 
1. **The Human Output**: What does the barista need to see on the cup?
2. **The Computer Output**: What data do we need to save so we can "remember" this customer next time?

## 📋 The Mandatory "Pantry" (Sprint 1 Requirements)
To pass your Sprint 1 Review, your Design Doc must account for these variables:

### 1. The Pricing Tier
* **Small**: $1.00
* **Medium**: $1.50
* **Large**: $2.00
* **Syrup Pumps**: $0.10 per pump

### 2. The Health & Safety Checklist (Milk Options)
You must support the following five options to ensure a "Hives-Free" experience:
* Dairy, Oat, Almond, **Soy**, and **Coconut**.

### 3. Delivery Logic
* You must collect a **Location** (Office Number or Department) to ensure the drink reaches the right desk.
